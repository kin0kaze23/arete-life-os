# Getting Started with Areté Life OS

Welcome to Areté Life OS - your personal encrypted vault for life management with AI-powered insights.

---

## First-Time Setup

### Step 1: Create Your Vault

When you first open the app, you'll see the "Create Secure Vault" screen.

1. **Choose a strong passphrase**
   - Minimum 8 characters
   - Mix uppercase + lowercase letters
   - Include numbers and symbols
   - Example: `MyLife2026!Secure` or `correct-horse-battery-staple`

2. **Confirm your passphrase**

3. **Click "Create Vault"**

> **Important:** Write down your passphrase somewhere secure. If you forget it, your data **cannot be recovered**.

### Step 2: Complete Onboarding

After creating your vault, you'll go through a brief onboarding:

1. **Your Name** - How the AI mentor will address you
2. **Basic Profile** - Optional info to personalize recommendations
3. **Review Settings** - Check your preferences

### Step 3: Explore the Dashboard

Once onboarded, you'll see the main dashboard with:

- **Mission Panel** - Your current focus
- **Neural Resonance** - AI coherence score
- **Goals** - Track your objectives
- **Insights** - AI-generated recommendations

## Core Concepts

### The Vault

Your vault is an encrypted container for all your personal data:

- **Encrypted locally** - Data never leaves your browser unencrypted
- **Passphrase protected** - Only you can access it
- **Auto-locks** - Locks after 15 minutes of inactivity

### Memory Items

These are pieces of information you share with the AI:

- Journal entries
- Life events
- Goals and aspirations
- Challenges and concerns

The AI processes these to provide personalized guidance.

### Claims & Facts

When you log memories, the AI extracts facts (claims) that update your profile:

- **Proposed** - AI suggests an update
- **Committed** - You've approved the update
- **Archived** - Rejected or outdated

### Rule of Life

Your personal operating system:

- **Season** - Current life phase and intensity
- **Values & Roles** - What matters to you
- **Weekly Rhythm** - Your recurring schedule
- **Non-Negotiables** - Sleep, rest, devotion time
- **Task Preferences** - Daily capacity and energy

## Quick Start Workflow

### Daily Routine (5 minutes)

1. **Unlock vault** with your passphrase
2. **Log a memory** - What's on your mind today?
3. **Review claims** - Approve or reject AI suggestions
4. **Check tasks** - See your recommended actions
5. **Lock vault** when done (or let it auto-lock)

### Weekly Review (15 minutes)

1. **Review goals** - Update progress
2. **Check insights** - Read AI recommendations
3. **Adjust Rule of Life** - Update if needed
4. **Export backup** - Save your encrypted vault

## Tips for Success

### Be Consistent

- Log something daily, even if brief
- The AI learns from patterns over time

### Be Honest

- The AI works best with accurate information
- Private thoughts stay encrypted and local

### Review Regularly

- Check claims before they pile up
- Adjust goals as life changes

### Backup Often

- Export your vault after major updates
- Store backups securely
