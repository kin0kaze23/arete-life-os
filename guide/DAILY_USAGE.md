# Daily Usage Guide

This guide covers how to use Areté Life OS effectively on a daily basis.

---

## Morning Routine

### 1. Unlock Your Vault

Open the app and enter your passphrase. The vault locks after 15 minutes of inactivity, so you'll need to unlock each session.

### 2. Review Your Daily Plan

Navigate to the Dashboard to see:

- **Today's Tasks** - AI-prioritized actions
- **Upcoming Events** - From your timeline
- **Active Goals** - Current objectives

### 3. Log Morning Intentions

Use the Memory Vault to capture:

```
Today I want to focus on [your priority].
I'm feeling [emotional state].
Key meetings/events: [list them].
```

The AI will process this and may suggest relevant tasks.

---

## Throughout the Day

### Capture Moments

When something significant happens:

1. Open Memory Vault
2. Type a quick note
3. The AI extracts facts automatically

**Examples:**

- "Had a productive meeting with the team about Q2 goals"
- "Feeling stressed about the upcoming deadline"
- "Finally finished reading that book on productivity"
- "Doctor said my blood pressure is normal"

### Attach Files (Optional)

You can attach:

- Documents
- Images
- Screenshots

The AI will analyze attached files and extract relevant information.

---

## Evening Routine

### 1. Review Claims

Check the Verification tab for pending claims:

- **Green checkmark** - Approve accurate facts
- **Red X** - Reject incorrect extractions
- **Edit** - Modify before approving

### 2. Update Timeline

Add significant events to your timeline:

- Achievements
- Milestones
- Important meetings
- Life events

### 3. Check Insights

Review AI-generated insights:

- **Recommendations** - Suggested actions
- **Blind Spots** - Areas you might be neglecting
- **Patterns** - Trends in your data

### 4. Log Reflection (Optional)

End the day with a brief reflection:

```
Today I accomplished [achievements].
Challenges faced: [list them].
Tomorrow I need to [priorities].
Gratitude: [what you're thankful for].
```

---

## Weekly Practices

### Sunday Evening (15 min)

1. **Review Week Summary**
   - What went well?
   - What could improve?

2. **Update Goals**
   - Mark progress
   - Adjust targets if needed

3. **Plan Next Week**
   - Use "Plan My Day" for the week ahead
   - Block time for priorities

4. **Export Backup**
   - Settings → Export Vault
   - Save to secure location

### Monthly (30 min)

1. **Rule of Life Review**
   - Is your current "season" accurate?
   - Update values if shifted
   - Adjust non-negotiables

2. **Deep Goal Review**
   - Are goals still relevant?
   - Create new goals as needed
   - Archive completed goals

3. **Profile Audit**
   - Check extracted facts
   - Correct any inaccuracies
   - Update stale information

## Troubleshooting

### Vault Won't Unlock

- Double-check your passphrase (case-sensitive)
- Try again slowly
- If forgotten, data cannot be recovered

### Session Locked Unexpectedly

- Normal behavior after 15 minutes of inactivity
- Just re-enter your passphrase

### AI Not Responding

- Check your internet connection
- Gemini API may have temporary issues
- Try again in a few minutes

### Data Not Saving

- Vault may have locked - check if you need to re-unlock
- Check browser storage (Settings → Storage Usage)
- Export backup if storage is near limit

## Best Practices

1. **Consistency > Intensity**
   - Log daily, even briefly
   - Small inputs compound over time

2. **Quality > Quantity**
   - Meaningful entries work better than verbose ones
   - The AI extracts what matters

3. **Review Regularly**
   - Claims pile up if ignored
   - Insights become stale

4. **Backup Often**
   - Weekly minimum
   - After major life updates

5. **Trust the Process**
   - The AI improves as it learns your patterns
   - Give it time to calibrate
