# AI Output Templates (Non-negotiable)

## Recommendation / Task (expanded detail)

- What to do: (1 line)
- Why it matters: (1 line, personalized)
- Steps: (3–7 ordered steps)
- Time & effort: (minutes + Low/Med/High)
- When to do: (suggested time window)
- Inputs needed: (docs/tools/info)
- Definition of done: (measurable)
- Risks/watch-outs: (1–3 bullets)
- Follow-up: (what gets logged/updated)
- Sources used: (links to Memory/Event/Profile signals)

## Watch-out / Risk item

- What to watch: (1 line)
- Why now: (trigger + horizon)
- Impact if ignored: (1 line)
- Next best prevention step: (action)
- Sources used: (signals)

## Plan My Day output

- Top 3 priorities (with full templates)
- Time-block schedule (morning/afternoon/evening)
- Buffer/rest block
- Blindside check (deadlines/risks)
- Needs Review queue (if missing info)
